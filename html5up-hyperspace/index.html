Who I am

Hi, I’m Brie—an innovative thinker, problem solver, and full-stack developer who loves creating seamless and user-friendly digital experiences. My background in entrepreneurship, design, and customer engagement gives me a unique perspective on building tech that actually connects with people.

My journey into coding started with a deep curiosity for how things work and a passion for bringing ideas to life. Running my own jewelry business, I handled everything from website design to social media marketing, which gave me firsthand experience in e-commerce, branding, and digital strategy. That experience naturally led me to tech, where I realized I love building, solving problems, and optimizing experiences.

Right now, I’m leveling up my skills in JavaScript, Node.js, React, and API integrations through Resilient Coders, where I’m learning to build scalable and interactive applications. My experience in freelance web development and hackathons has taught me how to think fast, collaborate, and turn ideas into reality.

I thrive in fast-paced, creative environments, love a good challenge, and am always looking for ways to grow. Whether it’s making a website run smoother, designing an intuitive interface, or building something from scratch, I’m all about creating tech with purpose.
